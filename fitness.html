Fitness Programs




Push

The push day focuses on exercises that primarily work the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Common exercises include bench press, shoulder press, and tricep extensions.

Pull

The pull day focuses on exercises that primarily work the back, biceps, and rear shoulders. Common exercises include pull-ups, rows, and bicep curls.

Legs

The leg day focuses on exercises that primarily work the lower body, including the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Common exercises include squats, lunges, and deadlifts.
